Our Philosophy.

Debate is a social network that focuses on intense conversations between users across the web. All the data is naturally obtained due to the system's user-driven data collection.

Hashtags.

Nowadays, hashtags play an important role in indicating and highlighting a viral topic. Therefore, we use hashtags to represent unique discussions, and of course they are all created by the users.

Hashtags are in the form #side.A_side.B, indicating two sides in a debate.

Identical hashtags can have different categories, which make them unique to the user.
